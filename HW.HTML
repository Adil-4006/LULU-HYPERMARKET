LULU HYPERMARKET (facts)

Global Chain: Lulu Hypermarket is part of the Lulu Group International, which operates numerous stores across the Middle East and other regions.

Diverse Product Range: It offers a wide selection of items, including fresh produce, groceries, electronics, clothing, home goods, and cosmetics.

Cultural Hub: The hypermarket features a mix of local and international products, catering to the diverse population of Bengaluru.

Food Court: Many Lulu Hypermarkets have food courts offering a variety of cuisines, making it a popular spot for dining as well as shopping.

Community Engagement: Lulu often engages in community events and promotions, supporting local producers and festive celebrations.

Sustainability Initiatives: The chain has implemented various sustainability practices, including waste management and sourcing local products.

Accessibility: Lulu Hypermarket aims to provide a convenient shopping experience with ample parking and easy access to public transport.


LuLu Group International is an Emirati-based multinational conglomerate company that operates a chain of hypermarkets and retail companies, headquartered in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.
It was founded in 2000 by M. A. Yusuff Ali from Nattika in Kerala, India.
